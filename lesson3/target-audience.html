Target Audience:

The target audinece for this site can be described as a general group of people who want to know the current weather and the predictions in our targeted area.


Personas:

  1. Farmers
  2. Adventurers

Scenarios

  • What will the weather be like for my camping trip?
  • Will it rain on my wedding day?
  • Will the baseball game get cancelled tomorrow?
  • What is th UV rating for this weekend?
  • Is the temperature going to get warmer this coming week?
